# Platform Overview

Blend now presents a premium, dark-themed event-platform homepage that highlights discovery, hosting, analytics, and community in a clear story arc:

- Hero: parallax background with overlay, primary CTA for app download, and secondary CTA to explore events.
- Value: four-card explanation of discovery, organizer tools, smart recommendations, and seamless ticketing/community.
- Features: grid covering discovery feed, organizer dashboard, analytics, secure payments, community modules, AI-powered curation, and automated reminders.
- Social Proof: testimonial slider plus partner logo strip for credibility.
- Final CTA: movement-focused message with dual buttons for download and exploration.
